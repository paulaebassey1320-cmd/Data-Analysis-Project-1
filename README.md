# Project Insights

Regional Performance:

West region leads in both sales and profit.

North region consistently records the lowest performance.

-  Sales Overview

Total Sales ≈ 11 Million

Electronics dominate regional sales.

Accessories follow closely.

Office category performs lowest.

- Profit Analysis

2024 records the highest profit, followed by 2023, then 2022.

Electronics generate the highest profit across all years.

Monthly profit peaks towards year-end (December).

- Product Performance

Monitor, Smartphone, and Camera are top-performing products.

Office-related products show weaker sales and profit contribution.

- Category Insights

Electronics: Highest revenue and profit driver.

Accessories: Moderate growth.

Office: Lowest contributor, needs strategic review.

# Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing focus on Electronics, especially in West and South regions.

- Investigate low performance in the North region (logistics, awareness, pricing).

- Improve or bundle Office products to boost demand.

- Stock more high-performing products like Monitors and Smartphones.

- Use seasonal trends (Q4 peak) for promotional campaigns.

  # Final Conclusion

The dashboard reveals that Electronics and the West region are the main revenue drivers of the business. Profit has steadily improved, peaking in 2024, with strong growth during the last quarter of the year.

However, Office products and the North region underperform, presenting opportunities for improvement through targeted marketing, pricing strategies, and operational optimization.

Overall, the dashboard provides a clear decision-support tool, enabling stakeholders to:

✔ Identify profitable regions

✔ Focus on top-performing products

✔ Optimize low-performing categories

✔ Plan seasonal sales strategies
